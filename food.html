Egg Masala

Bagara Egg Masala is a special curry that originated from the royal city of Hyderabad. It is indeed an improvised version of the dish 'bagara baingan'. This egg recipe tastes incredible when served with steamed or jeera rice.

Tandoori egg

Tandoori egg or egg tikka is devilled eggs coated with a variety of aromatic spices and onions, and served with mint chutney. In fact, tandoori eggs can also make your biryani exceptional and add that extra kick into it.

Nadan Kozhi Curry

Nadan Kozhi Curry is a traditional Kerala-style chicken curry which is made with succulent chicken chunks simmered in flavourful coconut oil or milk followed by some aromatic lightly roasted spices.
